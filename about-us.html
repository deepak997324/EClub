Entreprenuership Club - IIT Patna

About Us

"You could have a million ideas, but they're all worthless if you don't get them done"
Drawing inspiration from these words, the students at IIT Patna recently established the'Entrepreneurship Club' with the vision of creating a gelling point for aspiring entrepreneurs at the institute. The club has been set up to act as an umbrella organisation that coordinates all entrepreneurial activities at IIT Patna.

The Mission

The mission of the club is to educate themselves about the nuances involved in entrepreneurship and business in order to prepare them for undertaking the journey from the genesis of an idea to its successful business implementation. The students aim to foster an environment that would empower them to learn from each other and from the experiences of people who have undertaken such a journeyin their lives. The execution plan developed by the students includes organising lectures and workshops on business topics, inviting successful entrepreneurs and industry leaders for talks and speeches and organising intra-college activities to promote the spirit of entrepreneurship amongst the IITP community.





Logo Rationale

With an energetic and enterprising 'e' emanating a dazzling luminescence in a serene shade of sky blue, the logo emblematizes passion, zeal and innovation, inspiring trust and stability amongst fellow travellers. The design represents a metaphor to the Sun, the eternal source of all energy. The core radiates 'Steering Ideas' thereby spreading the apothegm that entrepreneurship is more a state of one's mind than anything else. Just as the sun nurtures life on earth, the Entrepreneurship Club strives to nurture the incipient ideas and entrepreneurial spirit that lie unexplored within all of us.
