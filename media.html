Management Edge to Hone Skills

Budding techies of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT)-Patna are picking up tricks of business planning, ad making and financial accounting alongside their regular lessons on fluid mechanics, thermodynamics, power generation and transmission. Almost two months after the entrepreneurship course was launched at IIT-Patna, third-year students find it interesting and different from the regular engineering concepts they learn. The elective course is only for those students who aim to start their own businesses like Infosys founder N.R. Narayana Murthy, HCL Technologies founder Ajai Chowdhry and others.

Steering Ideas : Entrepreneurship Club IIT Patna

"You could have a million ideas, but they're all worthless if you don't get them done"
Drawing inspiration from the words of Lauren Amarante, co-founder of World Entrepreneurship Day, the students of IIT Patna established an Entrepreneurship Club with the vision of creating a meeting point for aspiring entrepreneurs at the institute. The club has been set up to act as an umbrella organization that coordinates and bring together all entrepreneurial activities at Indian Institute of Technology, Patna.
