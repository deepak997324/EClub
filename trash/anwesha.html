Mr. Vikram Upadhyaya
Board Member
Indian Angel Network Incubator


Mr. Vikram Upadhyaya, Board Member, Indian Angel Network Incubator, visited IIT Patna during Anwesha'14, the techno-management festival of IIT Patna. He discussed about setting up Global Ventures and Franchising.
Mr. Vikram Upadhyaya is a technocrat turned serial entrepreneur holding a Masters Degree in Computer Science from the University of Tokyo, Japan and is an alumnus of the famous TRON laboratory. Vikram is the only Indian featured as Cover story in Keizaikai ( The economics magazine of Japan) . One of the most successful and influential indian Business Leaders in Japan and known for his success stories in India-Japan cross border collaborations.
